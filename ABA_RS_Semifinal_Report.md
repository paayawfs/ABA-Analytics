## Key Takeaway

The semifinals shook up the order. **Longshots** made the biggest leap — from .500 in the regular season to 60% overall, driven by Richard's dominance (35.5 EFF) and Boss Baeta's consistency. **Los Ashtros** fell hard, going from 62.5% to .500 with a -6.4 point differential. **AshKnights** stayed steady at the top. The story of this season is offense vs. defense: teams that can stop opponents (AshKnights, Longshots) win more than teams that just score (HillBlazers).
