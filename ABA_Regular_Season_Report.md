## Key Takeaway

The league has a clear top tier (AshKnights), a competitive middle (Los Ashtros, Longshots), and a bottom tier (HillBlazers, Berekusu Warriors). Turnovers weren't consistently tracked, so ball security comparisons aren't possible.
